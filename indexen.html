NAVIS / Internationalization

Over the past years sectors of the national economy have innovated by investing in the differentiation and quality of their products, with excellent results.
Today, sectors such as the textile, real estate, wine, cork, jewelry, footwear, agriculture, just to name a few, are able to compete in the global market.
NAVIS - National Agency for Valorization of International Sales – sees itself as an agency that provides an integrated, multidisciplinary internationalization service to companies.
The experience acquired during our professional careers enables us to determine that the companies’ major challenges are not only, in the production of goods or services, that are distinguished for quality and innovation, but also in placing those offers in potential markets.
This is particularly true when it comes to external markets.
Our primary purpose is to be the companies’ partner, providing knowledge and a team of multi-skilled technicians, and through an active partnership, to prepare them for the demands of the internationalization process.

Mission

Navis mission is to increase companies’ exports in quantity and value, thus, contributing to their strength and growth and consequently to the vitality of the national economy.

Vision

Navis vision is to be the companies’ natural partner whenever they wish to begin or consolidate their participation in the international market.
To attain recognition by being a reference of competence and know-how in the complex task of internationalizing a business, trademark, product or service.

Competitive advantage and Fees

The differentiation and competitive advantage of the Navis service lies in three determining elements that make up our DNA:

To provide a multi-sectorial service that handles the organization’s most profound and structural fragilities that are weakened when a process of internationalization is initiated and which reduce the chances of success.

The existence of a network of agents, recognized around the globe, that work exclusively for Navis clients scouting for international clients and advising on internationalization.

The sincerity by which we abide and the confidence that we have in our services, allows us to carry out a remuneration system exclusively based on the gains generated by the successful process of internationalization – a percentage, for a limited period, of the international business turnover, resulting from the Navis consultancy services.

For these reasons, we are convinced that we provide an innovative model of expert advice to companies. An exceptionally beneficial offer for all those who seek us.

How we work – Initial Contact

The business model of the Navis agency is founded on a philosophy that values transparency, simplicity, proximity and partnership with our clients.

Once we are contacted by a company, our approach is to schedule a meeting that allows our technicians to gather all the relevant information needed to prepare the internationalization process:

Knowledge of the product characteristics;
Existing distribution channels;
Previous media campaigns;
Intended export markets;
Actual production capacity and expansion possibilities (possible application for European Cohesion Funds of the NSR, which we also draw up);
Optimal production level in relation to production capacity;
Legal protection of trademark and innovative production processes;
Assessment of the possibility of optimizing the financial management;
Branding Work;
Other sectors that the company may wish to increase and optimize.

According to the results obtained in the preliminary analysis, a team of specialists in Marketing, Web Design, Operational Management, Financial auditing, Trademarks and Patent Rights shall work closely with the company’s management to decide on the strategic course of action to be followed.


How it works – Operational strategy

The preliminary report, which should be intuitive and very practical, will identify strategic and operational solutions that best suit the company. This will be a systematic and methodical tool which aim is to structure the course of action.

Suggestion of international markets that best fit the product’s specifications and positioning;
Construction or improvement of web-site;
Management of digital marketing in social networks;
Management of local media, whenever necessary, in foreign markets (radio, television, magazines and outdoors);
Management of investment sources and financial intermediation with public and private entities (banks, European cohesion funds – QREN and other community funds);
Management of brand´s representation in international trade fairs;
Management of the export process of goods: ports of departure and arrival, as well as distribution channels in the receiving markets, retail network, etc.;
Management of the protection of the trademark and original production processes (patents) through the legal channels so that it becomes recognized as a community and international trademark.

With the respective approval, the Navis technicians carry out the necessary strategic measures and handle the overall internationalization process.
